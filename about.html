A person who is strong-minded enough to wake up every day early and work on their hobby projects for several hours without interruption because they said so to themselfs. A person that always tries to finish what they've started. A person who prefers doing hard exercise or work against leaning back and watching TV or a movie. A person who tries to work hard on themselfs because they know that the effort is worth enough the great result afterwards. A person that tries their best to have an every day discipline and every day discomfort. And much more! Such person may probably have a really great mindset because all of this. They realizes how hard or how much effort they've put into something, how much energy it costs them, however enventually, they finished it, and that makes them feel happy.
